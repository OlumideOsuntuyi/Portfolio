  2. Chess Engine

    Unity 2D
    Opening Book
    Analysis

    ChessMaster AI is a high-performance chess engine developed in C# that analyzes chess positions at lightning speed, making it an essential tool for both amateur and professional players aiming to improve their game. It utilizes the NEGA-MAX algorithm to evaluate millions of positions, providing real-time insights and recommendations during gameplay. The engine integrates a comprehensive opening book that features probabilities of various positions based on extensive data from real-world games, offering strategic advice on optimal opening moves and counters.
