About Me

Olumide George Osuntuyi is a skilled game developer with a passion for creating immersive experiences through programming. Specializing in game development using C# and C++, he has extensive experience working with Unity and OpenGL to bring his visions to life. In addition to his game development expertise, Olumide is proficient in building .NET applications and working with shader languages, ensuring high-performance graphics and smooth gameplay. With a strong technical background and a creative mindset, he is dedicated to pushing the boundaries of interactive entertainment.

Voxel Craft

Unity 3D
HLSL
Reverse Engineering

Our reverse engineering project aims to recreate the beloved game Minecraft using the Unity game engine, enhancing it with advanced shader support for a more immersive visual experience. By leveraging Unity’s powerful rendering capabilities, we introduce dynamic lighting, realistic shadows, and vibrant textures that bring the blocky world to life like never before. This project not only replicates the core mechanics and gameplay of Minecraft but also pushes the boundaries of its graphical fidelity, offering players a fresh yet familiar adventure. Whether you’re exploring vast landscapes or building intricate structures, the enhanced shaders ensure every moment is visually stunning and engaging.
